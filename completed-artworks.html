Semi-realistic/Stylized Illustrations

WLOP Eye Study

I was on a mission to level up my art so I decided to do a quick sketch based on how wlop rendered his paintings. I eventually decided to stick to an eye since I was using my finger on a phone and rushed it since I wasn't in a good location to draw. I learnt a lot about rendering eyes from this drawing like how to draw attention to it and give it life.
